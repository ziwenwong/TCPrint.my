Company Introduction

PrintKing helps brands, shops and events prepare print-ready materials faster.

From stickers to banners and custom printing, we help businesses prepare clear, practical and production-ready print materials for daily sales, events and brand presentation.

What we print

Product labels, waterproof stickers, large format stickers, light box stickers, banner, bunting stand, poster, business card and other custom printing requests.

How we quote

Customers choose product, size, quantity and delivery. The calculator reads current prices from backend data, then prepares the WhatsApp order message.

How we follow up

Our backend keeps product options and pricing organised, so customers can get a cleaner quote flow while order details are prepared clearly for WhatsApp follow-up.
